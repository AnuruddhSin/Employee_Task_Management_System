About

The Employee Task Management System enables employees to register and request authentication from admins. Upon admin approval, employees receive their login credentials via email. The system allows admins to update employee details and passwords. Employees can also update their passwords. Admins can assign tasks to employees, and employees can delegate tasks to others. The query section allows employees to ask questions, which are tracked until resolved.

Features

Employee Registration

Employees register and request admin approval for authentication.

Email Notifications

Automated emails with login credentials after admin approval.

Profile Management

Admins and employees can update profiles and passwords.

Task Assignment

Admins assign tasks to employees; employees can delegate tasks.
